Why visit us?


Relax

Barista made coffee and other quality beverages in a relaxed and comfortable environment.

Through our regular yet informal chess events and competitions, meet new people who are also fans of the timeless game.

Borrow or Buy

Buy or borrow from our collection of in-store chess books and boards. Find the right ones to take your game to the next level.

Have Fun and Improve

Learning Chess is a fantastic way to relax, while learning about improvement in all areas of life. It takes plenty of practise, reflection and patience. Join us and let's have fun together.
